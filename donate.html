About GK Foundation Uganda

Founded by King VJ, GK Foundation Uganda provides a nurturing environment for 21 children, mostly girls. We ensure they have access to education, nutritious meals, healthcare, and daily essentials. Our mission is to raise them in dignity, hope, and love.

Our Goals

1) Education

Provide books, pens, and school uniforms so every child can attend school confidently.

2) Nutrition

Ensure three nutritious meals daily for healthy growth and learning.

3) Health & Hygiene

Provide healthcare, sanitary pads for girls, and daily hygiene supplies.
